Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much programming knowledge do I need to take this course?

No prior programming experience is required. Our course is designed for beginners and focuses on understanding AI concepts and prompt engineering principles. We teach you the necessary skills from the ground up.

2. What makes this course different from free online AI courses?

Unlike self-paced online courses, you'll learn directly from expert instructor Ali Cavdar, who has successfully taught 60,000+ students around the world. You'll also benefit from live interactive sessions, peer-to-peer learning, hands-on projects, and receive a Google AI Essentials certificate included in your course fee.

3. Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes! You'll receive two certificates: a completion certificate from Boo Academy and the Google AI Essentials certificate through Coursera (fully covered by us). These certifications demonstrate your practical AI skills and knowledge to potential employers.

4. What is the time commitment for this course?

The course runs for 6 weeks with two sessions per week. Each live session is approximately 2 hours, plus P2P practice sessions. We recommend setting aside 4-6 hours per week for coursework and practice. Sessions are scheduled to accommodate working professionals.

5. What can I do after completing this course?

Upon completion, you'll be equipped with practical skills to leverage AI in your professional journey. You can apply your knowledge to enhance business processes, create efficient workflows using AI tools, and develop innovative solutions using prompt engineering. Many of our graduates go on to implement AI solutions in their current roles or pursue new opportunities in AI-focused positions.
